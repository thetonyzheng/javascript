Our Story

We are engineers, data scientists, and insurance consultants with a mission to provide better business insurance for freelancers. We believe the future of work is distributed and decentralized, and the 60 million+ freelancers will be an integral catalyst in this paradigm shift. However, existing providers are inadequately built to serve this growing economy of freelancers and changing risk profiles. Not on coverage, not on experience, they simply do not understand freelancers. Our vision is to build a freelancer-first insurance provider by completely revamping the way risk is priced to offer better rates and flexible coverages all through a seamless digital interface. Join our story as we set out to revolutionize the insurance industry

Traditional providers aren't built to serve freelancers

Antiquated models

Risk models date back decades and don't represent changing risk profiles and working dyanmics

High operating costs

Complex network of brokers and middlemen drive cost by 15-25%, at minimum

Misaligned incentives

Brokers often sell higher premiums to hit quota bonuses from providers. Providers are motivated to increase premiums and limit claim payout

Slow processes

Layers of management and convoluted systems lead to sluggish responses and policy changes that take weeks
